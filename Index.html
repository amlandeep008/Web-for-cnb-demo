Introduction

A Brief Introduction of the Institution

With the conscious efforts of the citizens of the Bokakhat region, CNB Jr. College was started as a part of CNB College with Higher Secondary classes in 1997. The Junior College was part of its parent institution till 2002. Due to technical reasons and in accordance with the approval of AHSEC, CNB Jr. College was separated from its parent institution.

With the implementation of NEP 2020, the Department of Education, Government of Assam renamed all junior colleges of Assam as Senior Secondary Schools. During the last 26 years, CNB Senior Secondary School has focused on developing human resources along with upgrading its infrastructure.

The institution has made tremendous efforts to develop into a well-equipped educational institution offering both Science and Arts streams, incorporating elements from the contemporary scientific milieu. The Arts stream was introduced with permission from AHSEC from the session 2023 due to growing demand in higher secondary education and new education policies in the state.

The school has been continuously working to disseminate knowledge to its pupils with the solemn aim of making them worthy citizens of the country.

Rules & Welfare

Uniform, Identity Card, Rules & Regulations

College Uniform and Identity Card

Boys: Navy blue striped shirt on white background, navy blue trouser, navy blue tie with white stripes, formal black shoes, white socks, black belt, maroon sweater, and school-logo T-shirt on Wednesday & Saturday.

Girls: Navy blue striped shirt on white background (loose fitting), navy blue trouser, formal black shoes, white socks, black belt, maroon sweater, and school-logo T-shirt on Wednesday & Saturday.

All bonafide students will be given identity cards which must be worn every day. A new card after loss requires Rs. 70.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Mobile phones are strictly prohibited inside the school campus.
  2. Only licensed students above 18 years may bring a motor-cycle or scooty.
  3. Students must keep the campus and classrooms clean.
  4. Students must come in proper uniform and identity card.
  5. Ragging, tobacco, pan-masala, and indiscipline are strictly banned.
  6. Students must reach before 8:45 AM and attend morning assembly.
  7. School gate remains closed from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM.
Attendance Policy

Leave and Absence Guidelines

  1. All leaves must be obtained in advance.
  2. Leave application should mention the reason, period, name, class, and roll number.
  3. If absent for 15 days without intimation, the student's name may be struck off from records.
  4. Leave during school hours requires prior sanction; emergency cases may contact the office.
  5. Medical certificate/prescription is required for 2 or more days of leave.
  6. Medical fitness certificate is needed after infectious or contagious disease.
  7. 3-Day Excuse Rule: maximum excused absences of 3 days per month.
  8. Unexcused absence fine: Rs. 100 per day; late arrival: Rs. 50 per instance.
Parents

Parent Cooperation Matters

  1. Parents should ensure regular attendance and punctuality.
  2. School diary should be checked and countersigned regularly.
  3. Progress reports should be studied carefully and returned signed.
  4. Criticism of teachers or school in front of children should be avoided.
  5. Genuine complaints may be submitted in writing to the Principal.
  6. Grievances may be appealed to the Local Managing Board if needed.
  7. Enrolment implies willingness to comply with school requirements and regulations.
